About BlogHaven

A modern blogging platform focused on clarity, writing, and meaningful interaction.

What is BlogHaven?

BlogHaven is a minimalist blogging platform built to give writers a clean and distraction-free space to share their thoughts, ideas, and experiences.

The platform prioritizes readability, simplicity, and thoughtful design over unnecessary features and clutter.

Key Features

  • Write and publish posts with a clean editor
  • Organize content using genres
  • Like posts and engage with content
  • Comment system for discussions
  • Light and dark mode for comfortable reading
  • Responsive design for mobile and desktop

Why BlogHaven?

BlogHaven was built as a full-stack Django project with a focus on modern UI, accessibility, and real-world development practices.

It serves both as a functional blogging platform and as a learning project that demonstrates clean architecture, user authentication, and interactive features.
